# PRODUCT.md — CampusSync Edge

## Register
**Product** — authenticated SaaS dashboard where users are in-task. The tool should disappear into the task.

## Users
Engineering students in India (and globally) preparing for placement season. Ages 18-24. Technical but not designer-level. They want clarity, not flash.

## Purpose
AI-powered career readiness intelligence: resume analysis, skill gap detection, readiness scoring, interview preparation, improvement planning, and project verification.

## Brand Personality
- **Professional** — this is a career tool, not a game
- **Trustworthy** — data-driven, accurate, reliable
- **Efficient** — fast to scan, quick to act on
- **Empowering** — students feel confident after using it

## Anti-References
- ❌ Neon glow effects, cyber/HUD aesthetics
- ❌ Emoji as icons (use SVG: Lucide)
- ❌ Gradient text on headings
- ❌ Glassmorphism as default surface style
- ❌ Overly complex loading animations (the old "INITIALIZING ENGINE..." sequence)
- ❌ 7000-line single CSS files
- ❌ Pure black (#000) or pure white (#fff) backgrounds

## Strategic Design Principles
1. **Familiarity over novelty** — use standard SaaS patterns (sidebar, cards, tables)
2. **Content over decoration** — every pixel should serve a purpose
3. **Accessible by default** — 4.5:1 contrast, keyboard nav, reduced motion
4. **Fast by default** — code-split, lazy-loaded, minimal JS for non-interactive pages
