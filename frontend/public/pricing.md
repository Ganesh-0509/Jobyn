# Pricing — Jobyn

Jobyn is **free** for engineering students. There are no paid tiers and no credit card is required.

## Free
- **Price:** $0
- **Who it's for:** Engineering students preparing for placements / job applications.
- **Included (everything):**
  - Resume readiness scoring (ML, ~95% accuracy) for 7 career roles
  - Auto role detection + cross-role best-fit recommendation
  - Skill-gap analysis with dependency-aware study plans
  - AI study tutor (RAG-powered tutorials, quizzes, LeetCode practice)
  - Voice-based AI mock interviews with concept-coverage scoring
  - AI project generator + GitHub project verification
  - Progress tracking, gamification, and a shareable readiness certificate
  - Privacy mode: on-device (in-browser) resume scoring — no data leaves your device
- **Limits:** None for individual student use. Quick Score works with no signup.
